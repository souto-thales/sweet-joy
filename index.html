Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

We require a minimum of 5 days notice for custom cakes. For elaborate designs, wedding cakes, or quinceañera cakes, we recommend ordering 2–4 weeks in advance to ensure we can dedicate the time your creation deserves. That said, life doesn't always follow a schedule — if you have an urgent need, please reach out. We'll do everything we can to make it work, because every customer matters to us.

Do you deliver to Winston-Salem, Greensboro, and High Point?

Yes! We serve the entire Triad area including Kernersville, Winston-Salem, Greensboro, and High Point, NC. Contact us via WhatsApp or the order form to confirm availability and arrange delivery for your event.

What is a brigadeiro?

A brigadeiro is Brazil's most beloved confection — a soft chocolate truffle made with condensed milk, butter, and cocoa, hand-rolled and finished with sprinkles or other toppings. Our gourmet brigadeiros come in 17 flavors, from classic chocolate to pistachio, salted caramel, Oreo, passion fruit, and more.

Can I customize the filling, size, and design of my cake?

Absolutely! Choose from 4 sizes (4" to 12"), vanilla or chocolate batter, and 11 filling options — white chocolate, three-milk, caramel, strawberry, walnut praline, and more. The decoration, colors, and theme are fully customizable.

Do you make cakes for weddings and quinceañeras?

Yes! We create elegant, show-stopping cakes for weddings, quinceañeras, baby showers, graduations, birthdays, and all milestone events. For multi-tier or elaborate designs, we recommend placing your order at least 3–4 weeks ahead.
